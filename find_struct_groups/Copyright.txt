The data for disease network was taken from K.-I. Goh, M. E. Cusick, D. Valle, B. Childs, M. Vidal, and A.-L. Barabási. The human disease network. 104(21):8685–8690, 05 2007.

The data for football network was downloaded from M. Newman's website and converted to an appropriate format.  See M. Girvan and M. E. J. Newman, Community structure in social and biological networks, Proc. Natl. Acad. Sci. USA 99, 7821-7826 (2002).

The data for karate network was downloaded from M. Newman's website and converted to an appropriate format.  Original data source:  W. W. Zachary. An information flow model for conflict and fission in small groups. J. Anthro. Res., 33(4):452–473, 1977.
